  • About

    Plog Hub is a blogging platform for developers. If you're looking for an analogy : Medium for developers.

    Here's a quick overview of the features of Plog Hub:

    • Write programming related blogs using a subset of markdown.
    • Comment on blogs posts by other users.
    • The front page helps you discover trending blog posts.

    What are the edit and delete policies on comments and blog posts?

    • Comments can be edited for 2 hours from the time of being posted provided there are no replies to the comment.
    • Comments can be deleted within 2 hours from the time of being posted provided there are no replies to the comment.
    • Blog posts can be edited anytime as there is no time limit.
    • Blog posts can be deleted within 24 hours of being posted provided there are no comments on the post.
    • If you want a comment or blog post to be altered beyond the mentioned time limits, please send an email explaining your case, the moderators will review your case.

    Which markdown tags are supported?

    • Supported in the comments : ['a','p','blockquote', 'code', 'em', 'strong', 'li','ol','ul', 'pre']
    • Supported in blog posts = ['a','p','blockquote', 'code', 'em', 'strong', 'li','ol','ul', 'h1','h2','h3','h4','h5','h6', 'pre']

    If you are new to markdown, refer to this guide.
